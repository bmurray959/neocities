Lore
Arka used to work in a traveling circus. She made good friends with the orcs there, even learning Orcish! She passed trials to be in service of a king of a kingdom, including getting her & her companions getting trounced by goblins, and fighting a dragon turtle thing. After defeating the turtle her and her companions won treasure! And she was cursed with a ring that makes her sink into the water. GOOD THING SHE CAN BREATHE UNDERWATER.
